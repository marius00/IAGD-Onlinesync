This is the Sign-In for Online Backups for Item Assistant.
The Sign-In is available via Live/Outlook Sign-In, Google Sign-In and Facebook.

By using this service, the following details will be stored about you:
  • Your e-mail address
  • The data required to recreate your Grim Dawn items
  • The date/time each item were uploaded
  • Your IP address for up to 24 hours (for security purposes and throttling excess traffic)
The information will be stored encryptet in Microsoft Azure (EU)
No information will be shared with any third parties unless legally obligated by law.

If you wish to permanently delete your items and stop using Item Assistant, please contact itemassistant@gmail.com
